Here's a time-line of his life...

A very simple person who lived in an unpretentious lifestyle. He had a keen interest in literature and wrote poems. He never married , He always his failures while let others address for the success. He remained active till the very end. He died while delivering a lecture at hte IIM

  • 1931: Born on October 15 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1954: Graduated from Saint Joseph's College in Tiruchirappalli with a degree in Physics
  • 1958: Graduated from the Madras Institute of Technology with a degree in Aeronautical Engineering
  • 1960: Joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)
  • 1969: Joined the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) as the project director of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • 2015: Passed away on July 27 after collapsing during a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management in Shillong
